Wordtracker vs Google Keyword Planner

The Google keyword research tool is the 'Keyword Searcher'. It's designed for SEO, so competition and other metrics are given only for paid search. Numbers are scaled from a sample, and similar keywords are grouped together. Instead, use a tool built for keyword research.

Our own proprietary data and toolset means you can get 10,000 keywords per search as well as related keywords, so your pages can be optimised more effectively to outrank the competition.
